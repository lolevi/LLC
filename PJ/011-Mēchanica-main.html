Mēchanica

To the Mechanical Era but not those electronic stuff

Mēchanica is a camera bag which is designed for holding mechanical film cameras and manual lens. The upper partition is made for keeping film rolls, a mechanical timer, a mechanical shutter wire and a selenium light meter.

Design

From the style concept of harmony between the "Mobile Camera-Container" and "Old Mechanical Film Camera", Mēchanica's form & shape were inspired by those good old day mechanical designs.

Technical Drawings

Materials

Black genuine Leather
Black wax thread
Brass magnet buttons

Craft Work

Cutting and stitching leather had to be very accurate as the design was in geometic style. This craft work is dedicated to the mechanical era bringing the most harmonic design and inspiration to me.
