The Design for Elegance

Elegance was a local beauty company. The logo design was the first priority from the client side. It symbolizes the concept values of the brand. The lady closes her eyes with a faint smile crept across is the moment of grace. Her coloured layered rich hair contrasting the face and the clear shoulder show the minimalism posture of her. The small round earring symbolizes the refined style. Logo is mainly outlined by circular lines, which preserves the peaceful moment of the woman with inner beauty.
